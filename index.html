Favorites

I like Eskabeche. It’s the kind of dish that feels like home, no matter where you are. The smell of it cooking brings back so many memories—quiet dinners with family, festive gatherings, or even just a comforting meal after a long day. There’s something magical about the way its flavors come together in perfect harmony.But what I love most about Chicken Adobo isn’t just the taste—it’s the story it tells. It reminds me of my roots, of family meals filled with laughter and stories. It’s a dish that connects generations, passed down from one cook to the next, each adding their own twist. Chicken Adobo isn’t just food; it’s comfort, tradition, and love all served on a plate. And no matter how many times I have it, it never loses its charm.
